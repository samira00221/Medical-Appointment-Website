ABOUT US

Baho International Hospital is a modern healthcare facility located in Kigali, Rwanda. It offers a wide range of medical services including general medicine, specialized care, and advanced diagnostics. With a commitment to high-quality and affordable healthcare, the hospital serves both local and international patients. Baho Hospital is known for its professional staff and patient-centered approach. Its mission is to promote wellness and ensure accessible medical care for all.
